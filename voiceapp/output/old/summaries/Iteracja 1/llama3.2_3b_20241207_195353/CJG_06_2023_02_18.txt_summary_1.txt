1. Opportunities for economic growth through sustainable energy sources and reducing dependence on fossil fuels.
2. Risks of ignoring environmental issues leading to severe health consequences, social unrest, and political instability.

**Conclusion:**

The text highlights the pressing issue of environmental degradation in Poland, particularly air pollution caused by fossil fuels. Ignoring this problem will have severe economic, health, social, and political consequences, including increased poverty, mortality rates, stress, and potential mental health crises. It is essential for Poland to prioritize sustainable energy sources, reduce its dependence on cheap energy from abroad, and take action to mitigate the impact of pollution on public health and the economy.
