  - **Policy Reforms**: Regulatory changes could be implemented to ensure that credit policies do not disproportionately affect certain groups.
- **Risks**:

  - **Social Unrest**: Widening inequality due to financial exclusion could result in social unrest if not addressed appropriately.

#### 5. Conclusion
The text emphasizes the importance of fostering societal wisdom and improving individual financial knowledge to make better decisions. Future considerations should include:
- **Financial Literacy Programs**: Developing comprehensive educational programs to equip individuals with essential economic knowledge.
- **Policy Review**: Regularly reviewing and adjusting credit policies to ensure they are fair and supportive of long-term economic stability.

By addressing these insights, policymakers can work towards creating a more financially resilient and inclusive society.
