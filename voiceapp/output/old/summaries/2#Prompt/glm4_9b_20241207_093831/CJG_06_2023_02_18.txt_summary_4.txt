- **Opportunities**: Transitioning to renewable energy sources could improve Poland's economic stability, reduce health issues, and contribute to global environmental goals.
- **Risks**: Continuing reliance on fossil fuels and environmental degradation pose significant risks to public health, economy, and social stability.

**5. Conclusion:**
The text calls for immediate attention to the unsustainable use of fossil fuels and illegal waste disposal practices in Poland. It suggests that these issues are not only economic but also have profound implications for public health and social well-being. A shift towards sustainable practices is seen as critical to avoid a potential economic, environmental, and social catastrophe. The text emphasizes the importance of international cooperation and political will to address these challenges.
